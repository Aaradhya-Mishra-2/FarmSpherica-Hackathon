The process

How Farmspherica works

Three steps from seed to harvest — all tracked, all optimised.

🌱
STEP 01

Plant your crops

Choose from 40+ supported hydroponic crops. Add them to your system and Farmspherica immediately starts building a personalised grow profile.

📊
STEP 02

Monitor in real time

Sensors stream pH, EC, temperature and humidity to your dashboard every 30 seconds. Smart alerts flag anything outside optimal range immediately.

🚀
STEP 03

Harvest with confidence

Progress bars show exact grow percentages per crop. You'll know harvest day before it arrives — and yield data builds over each cycle.
